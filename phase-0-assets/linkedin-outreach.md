# LinkedIn Outreach Templates

---

## Template 1: Pain-Based (Connection Request)

**Use when:** You've observed a specific inefficiency or manual process at their company.

> Hi [FIRST_NAME] — noticed [COMPANY] is scaling the ops team. Usually means a flood of manual workflows that AI could handle. Would love to connect.

**Character count:** ~160

---

## Template 2: Pain-Based (InMail)

**Use when:** You can reference a specific observation about their workflow or tech stack.

> Hi [FIRST_NAME],

> I noticed [OBSERVATION] at [COMPANY] — that's a pattern I see a lot in growing ops teams. Usually means 15-20 hours/week buried in manual triage, status updates, or report formatting that could be automated.

> I run focused AI audits that map out exactly where those hours are hiding and what to do about it. No generic "AI strategy" — just a concrete action plan for your specific stack.

> Would it be worth a 20-minute call to see if there's a fit?

---

## Template 3: Curiosity-Based (Connection Request)

**Use when:** No specific observation, but the person's role suggests automation opportunities.

> Hi [FIRST_NAME] — I help ops leaders at companies like [COMPANY] automate the repetitive work that eats their week. Happy to share what I'm seeing in the space.

**Character count:** ~180

---

## Template 4: Curiosity-Based (InMail)

**Use when:** Targeting someone who'd benefit but you have no specific hook.

> Hi [FIRST_NAME],

> Quick question — how much time does your team spend on ticket triage, status updates, and pulling reports across tools each week?

> I ask because I've been helping ops teams at similar-sized companies automate those exact workflows. One recent client cut their daily support triage from 45 minutes to under 2 minutes.

> I'm not selling software — I audit your existing tools and build automations on top of what you already use (Slack, Jira, Notion, etc.).

> Worth a quick chat to see if there's anything worth automating on your end?

---

## Template 5: Warm Intro / Referral

**Use when:** You share a mutual connection or the person was referred to you.

> Hi [FIRST_NAME] — [MUTUAL_CONNECTION] suggested we connect. I help operations teams automate manual workflows using AI, and they mentioned [COMPANY] might have some opportunities there.

> I'd love to hear what your team's workflow looks like — even if it's just a 15-minute call to swap notes. No pitch, just a conversation.

**Character count:** ~290
